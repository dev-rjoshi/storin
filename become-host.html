About Storin

What is Storin?

Storin connects people with places to store luggage and explore the places from around the world. The community is powered by hosts, who provide their guests with the freedom to safely store their luggage.

What is hosting?

You can earn money by sharing your unused space to store the luggage of travellers from around the world. When you host and how you interact with your guests is completely up to you.

How can I get started?

To be a host, you need to run a business with storage space for at least 5 bags. The most successful hosts have late hours and/or are close to transport hubs or tourist attractions

What's in it for me?

At Storin we aim to connect local shops and hotels who have unused secure space with tourists who need somewhere to store their bags. Storin is a wonderful way to monetize this spare space. You make money on every bag you store, right from the first bag, and there are no set up fees.

What do I need to do next?

Storin takes care of everything in terms of organization and administration. You'll receive email updates of when bookings come in, and you can check them online on your dashboard, and all you need to do is follow the security procedure when a customer drops their luggage with you. This involves checking their booking reference, government-issued photo ID, asking security questions and storing their bags safely until they return. We'll send you a Welcome Pack with everything you need.

Is there a vetting process?

Security is our top priority at Storin. It is equally important for us, for customers, and for hosts to have confidence in our business and running smooth operations. So, hosts will only be approved and featured on our website once they have passed our checks. These checks include but not limited to having a secure storage space that is only available to your staff, space is lockable and there is always a member of staff available to monitor, the Storin team must be satisfied that you are able to competently run the bag drop and collection process. We are looking for friendly staff who will give customers confidence in using Storin and encourage them to return. We periodically run inspections of our hosts to ensure operations are up to standards. We respond quickly to any feedback less than 5-star. Hosts found to be negligent and not up to our standard will be given one final warning, before being dropped from our network.
