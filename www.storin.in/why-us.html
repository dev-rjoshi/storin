Why Storin

The major concerns of any Managed Service Provider(MSP), IT Support, Data Center, Cloud Solution Provider and other IT Solution Providers is to decide whether they can safely outsource or not? Whether companies overcharge? what will be the productivity? Whether Outsource will make troubles than help?

Well, Storin’s IT industry experience and vast knowledge in Outsourcing, we tried to avoid all major pitfalls of Outsourcing such as Cost, Time Factor, Familiarity with Client Systems, Employee Relationship, Liability, Productivity, Priority, Morale, Availability and Control. These all major concerns is eliminated in our “Dedicated IT Team” Model. This model not just reduce IT Operational costs but also increase productivity, Greater Availability of Talents and Greater Workforce Flexibility for scale up or down the team size as required.

Since our 100% concentration on Dedicated IT Team Model, our primary focus is to build a long lasting relationship with the partner company and grow parallel with our partners growth. In this model we engage with any small, medium or corporate IT Service provider to build their team with us as a second and geographically separated office which will comply with 24/7 support requirements for zero down time support services with full control over the team as the one in your office.
