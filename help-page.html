Questions? You're in the right place.

How does Storin work?

We work with a network of local business who have secure extra space. You must place bookings on our website. Once you’ve booked you’ll get a confirmation e-mail. When you drop off your luggage at the host, show them booking id and government issued ID.

Is it safe?

Yes, absolutely. All our hosts are vetted by Storin and have a secure area for storing your luggage. You will receive a uniquely numbered security tag for each bag.

Where can I find a host to store my luggage?

With thousands of locations worldwide, Storin is everywhere. Simply type in your location into the search bar, and we will automatically search locations for you. If you didn't find any host in your city, write to us at cs-reply@storin.in and we'll find you one. If your business would make a good host, get in touch.

What can I store with Storin?

Our focus is your happiness, but we can store your bags, suitcases, bikes, sports equipment and strollers too. Let us know if you have a request and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Can I leave my passport and other travel documents in my bag?

We recommend not to store your passport or travel documents in your bag. Keep all your travel documents with you.

Can I store my valuable items?

We recommend that valuables, including but not limited to electronics and travel documents, are stored at your own risk. Our terms and conditions prohibit storage of certain items, including cash and securities. Please see them for more details.

Do I need to book in advance?

All bookings must be done online but bookings are valid as soon as they are placed, so you can book them last minute too.

Does the booking have to be made online?

All bookings must be done online through our website for security purposes. The process only takes 53 seconds.

I'll be late, can I still collect my luggage?

We understand things happen. The drop-off and pick up times are only intended as an estimate. Just double check the host's opening hours, and if your booking is within the length of time you paid for, and there won't be any problems.

How much does it cost?

Storin's aim is to provide safe convenient storage for everyone. The price is just INR 200 per item per day, see pricing page for more details. Our hosts are located just a few minutes walk from all main tourist attractions and transport hubs, and cost over 50% less.

Can I store my luggage for longer than a few days?

If you require long term storage, get in touch with us on cs-reply@storin.in and we'll get back to you with a discounted quotation.

What do I need to bring with me on the day?

You must bring the booking ID, the government issued ID and of course your luggage. You will be given a ticket that matches with the security tag attached to your bag. When you collect your luggage, bring this counterpart and your government issued ID.

Is there a size limit on what I can store?

We charge only by a number of bags no matter the size and weight. Get in touch with us on cs-reply@storin.in if you have any specific requirement, we'll do our best to accommodate.

I don't need to store my bags anymore, can I get a refund?

We have a flexible refund policy. Please send us an e-mail on cs-reply@storin.in with your booking ID and we'll get that done for you. Please see our cancellation and refund policy for more details.

My plans have changed, can I change my booking?

Please send us an e-mail on cs-reply@storin.in with your booking ID and the changes you want to make, we'll help you out from there.

Can I drop something off for someone else to pick up?

We do not allow anyone except the account owner to drop off and pick up luggage for security reasons. If you have a problem, please send us an e-mail on cs-reply@storin.in

The host is closed what should I do?

We're very sorry, sometimes things go beyond our control. If this is the case, call us straight away on +91 xxx xxx xxxx. We will:

  • Contact the host to find out how soon they can open, or
  • If we can't reach them, arrange for new storage.
  • If neither is possible, we will refund you for the inconvenience.

Please do take careful note of the opening hours, if you arrive after the host has closed to collect your luggage, there is not a lot we can do, but we always do our best to make your travel more enjoyable.
