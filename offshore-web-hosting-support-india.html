Offshore Web Hosting Support

Be it a small, medium or large scale web hosting company, support is a key aspect in the the long term success of the business. As a reputed and trustworthy offshore web hosting support provider in India, Storin offers 24/7 web hosting support to ensure a hassle free hosting experience. We provide a multi-tiered hosting support plan including shared, semi-dedicated and dedicated plans based on your requirements.

Our managed hosting services effectively covers 24/7 monitoring and management of servers. For small and medium businesses, dedicated managed hosting is the right solution. The dedicated managed hosting services provided by Storin include hardware and software setup, vulnerability scans, detecting intrusions, firewall updates, monitoring, data backups and more.

Shared plans:

Shared plans are volume based plans in which the team will work on multiple clients at any point of time. These are effectively support desk based plans with varying rates depending upon the number of tickets received during a specific timeframe.

  • 24/7 Support.
  • Highly cost efficient.
  • Shared between multiple clients.
  • Meant for small scale hosting services.

Semi-dedicated

  • Cost-effective.
